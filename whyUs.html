Our Mission

Giveroo is built from the heart, driven by a passion for growing strong teams that bring in long-lasting donors. We believe the real impact of fundraising begins after the first year, and we’re focused on building meaningful relationships and long-term giving that truly makes a difference.

What Makes Us Different

We invest in our fundraisers like no one else — through training, mentorship, and meaningful support.

Long-Term Results

We don’t chase quick wins. We focus on building high-retention donor relationships that last.

Brand-Safe Partnerships

Your reputation is our priority. We uphold the highest standards in every interaction.

Nationwide Experience

Meet the Founders

Jesse Hohnstein – Co-Founder, Giveroo

Jesse has been in the face-to-face fundraising world for over 8 years, starting at Global Faces Direct where he met his two best friends — and now business partners — Nic and Jordan. He’s known for his hands-on leadership, sharp eye for talent, and passion for people.

He’s a savage at promoting others and loves helping underdogs rise — taking people who are struggling and showing them how to turn things around by making the business a lifestyle. For Jesse, it’s more than just fundraising — it’s about connection, growth, and building a family. He’s proud to be creating a place where people can succeed — and feel at home.

Jordan Nur – Co-Founder, Giveroo

I’m in fundraising for the people — not just the ones our partner charities support, but the people doing the work on the ground every day. I’m passionate about building teams that uplift each other, keeping fundraisers focused on the bigger picture, and helping them grow through the hard days. There’s nothing better than watching someone turn rejection into resilience and reconnect with why they do this work.

Nicolas Gilbert – Co-Founder, Giveroo

Nicolas has been immersed in the fundraising world since 2019. What started as a job for him quickly became a passion — one that’s allowed him to represent over twenty incredible charities and help secure thousands of long-term monthly donors for causes that truly matter to him and the generous donors he’s had the privilege of interacting with.

Throughout the years, he’s had the privilege of training thousands of new fundraisers and leading successful face-to-face fundraising teams across British Columbia, Alberta, and Ontario. He believes in the power of purpose-driven work — not just for the impact it has on the world, but for the way it transforms the people doing it.

What fuels Nicolas every day is the opportunity to build strong teams, develop new leaders, and mentor individuals who are just beginning their journey in the fundraising industry. Watching someone grow their confidence, master their craft, and eventually lead their own team is one of the most rewarding parts of what he loves doing.

At the heart of it all, Nicolas is driven by a simple goal: to make a meaningful difference — for the charities he and his teams serve, the donors they connect with, and the fundraisers he inspires along the way.
